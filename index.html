About Me

I am currently working towards completing my PhD at the university of Amsterdam. My research focuses on automating regulatory constraints for healthcare research.I hold a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and specialized in security and privacy during my master's studies. I am from Ethiopia, I have a passion for exploring diverse cultures through travel.

Research Interests

Techno-Legal Privacy Research
