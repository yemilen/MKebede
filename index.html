M.G. Kebede

Exploring the intersection of technology, law, and privacy

About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Amsterdam. My research focuses on automating regulatory constraints for healthcare research. I am supervised by Prof.Dr. Tom van Engers who is a professor at the Faculty of Law and Faculty of Science at the University of Amsterdam.

Research Interests

My work aims to bridge the gap between technology and legal frameworks to enhance privacy and security of personal data.
